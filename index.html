    Exclusive Range Of Furniture

    Agarwal Furnico is a unit of Agarwal Industries estd. in 1974 as a leading furniture house in the district of Nagaon as well as Assam. We deal in various wooden and steel furniture for Home, Office, Institutions, Auditorium and Hospitals.

    Agarwal Furnico is focused on providing high-quality service and customer satisfaction and we will do everything we can to meet your expectations and needs. With a variety of designs and brands to choose from, we are sure you will be happy to associate with us.

    We are committed to offering our customers the widest range in home furniture, at the most affordable prices. From King and Queen sized beds, wardrobes, dressing tables for bedroom, sofa sets, chairs, tables for living room to crockery cabinets, chest of drawers for your kitchen and office. Our range extends in providing furnitures for Salons and outdoors. We go to great lengths to ensure that we provide only the best quality, so that it is easy and hassle free to buy furniture.
